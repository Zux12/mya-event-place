About Mya Event Place

Mya Event Place is a cosy yet versatile event hall located at Street Mall, Cyberjaya, 63000 Cyberjaya, Selangor. Surrounded by shops, cafés and an attractive pondside walkway, it is an ideal venue for both formal and informal occasions.

The hall can be configured for classroom, theatre, banquet or open space layouts – suitable for corporate trainings, product launches, akad nikah, mini receptions, birthdays, reunions, workshops and more.

  • Approx. multi-purpose hall with flexible setup
  • Air-conditioned indoor space with lobby and outside hall area
  • Pondside view and brick pathway for photos & outdoor activities
  • Nearby surau, F&B outlets and public facilities
Flexible layout – empty hall ready to match your event concept.
